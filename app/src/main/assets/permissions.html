This App. requires following permissions:

  • Location: When there's an Emergency, you can quickly send your location to your favourite ones.

  • Send Sms: A quick sms will be sent to your favourite contacts with your current location coordinates.

  • Contacts: You can select your favourite contacts whom you can call or quickly contact when there is an emergency.

  • Storage: To store temporary files on your device like cookies, session information etc.

  • Telephone: To directly call your favourite contacts you have added.


    • Disclaimer: We don't store your location information or contacts anywhere, they are stored on your internal storage, you can always opt out if you don't want to allow any permission. For more details, visit our Privacy Policy page.
