Pocket Doctor (v 1.1)

Your Emergency Doctor, help kit and medicine remainder.



Pocket Doctor is powerful tool inside your pocket. It knows everything about you. It is an emergency kit and more like a remainder for your medicines. You can save all your symptoms,their treatments, medicines that you have ever consumed or using currently all in one place. You don't need to remember anything now.

Getting Started:

  • Emergency Contacts : You can add emergency contacts who are living/residing near you, once you add these contacts, you can quickly call them without searching in the contact list for help. Just click on the call button in the app's main screen and add some.


  • Emergency Sms : Once you have added emergency contacts, you can sent a sms to all of them at once which may contain your quick message and your current location url.


  • Quick Emergency Message : You can also set a predefined sms message, to add one, Go to Settings and add a quick sms message. This message will be included in the sms you sent to your contacts followed by your location url.


  • Emergency Alert : A loud alert message will continue to ring if there's an emergency (even if your phone is on Silent mode). You can also customize the time after which it will repeatedly ring.


  • Allergies : You can add all things whom you are allergic to, their symptoms and treatment. It may help you or your friends in future.


  • Medications : It may contain all the prescriptions and precautions you need to follow. You can also add some of your recent medicines you use for future reference.


  • Histories : You can add all the information or describe what you have been through in prior or recently. It will be a great memory.
